Smart Traffic Management

Enhancing Urban Mobility

Our advanced traffic management system leverages real-time data to provide dynamic traffic monitoring and improve urban mobility.

  • Real-Time Traffic Data

    Monitor live traffic conditions using real-time data from the HERE API. Our system displays traffic status within a customizable radius, up to 10 km, on an interactive map.

  • Interactive Traffic Map

    The interactive map shows traffic conditions using polylines, allowing users to see congestion levels and traffic flow. By default, the system shows traffic within a 1 km radius, which can be adjusted as needed.

  • Comprehensive Traffic Analysis

    Get detailed information on nearby routes, including live feed data, route names, business relevance, and jam factors. This data helps users make informed decisions about their commute and travel plans.
